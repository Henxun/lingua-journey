# AI Teacher & Assessment System

## Why

Language learning platforms need more than just vocabulary drills and conversation practice. Learners benefit from:

1. **Personalized AI Tutoring** - One-on-one AI teacher that explains grammar, provides contextual examples, and adapts to the learner's level
2. **Progress Assessment** - Continuous evaluation of listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills to track improvement
3. **Intelligent Feedback** - Not just "wrong" but WHY it's wrong and HOW to improve
4. **Adaptive Learning** - AI that understands where the learner struggles and focuses on weak areas

## What Changes

### AI Teacher Features
- **Personalized Lessons**: AI-generated explanations tailored to user's level and learning goals
- **Grammar Explanations**: Deep grammar explanations with examples in context
- **Vocabulary in Context**: Teaching words through sentences and real-world usage
- **Cultural Insights**: Cultural nuances and idioms that textbooks miss
- **Interactive Q&A**: Answer follow-up questions about confusing topics

### Assessment System Features
- **Comprehensive Testing**: Evaluate listening, speaking, reading, writing skills
- **CEFR Alignment**: Map progress to international standards (A1-C2)
- **Skill Breakdown**: Detailed analysis of each language skill
- **Progress Tracking**: Historical performance and improvement trends
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Suggest areas needing more practice
